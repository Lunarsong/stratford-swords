About Us

Stratford Swords is a friendly and inclusive group based in the lovely Shakespearean town of Stratford-upon-Avon and focused on the study of Historical Fencing.

We study manuscripts written by Medieval and Renaissance fencing masters, such as Johannes Liechtenauer, Fiore dei Liberi, and Salvator Fabris, who wrote their instructions for fighting with the different weapons of the day.

Our school aims to create a welcoming and collaborative space. We examine a variety of weapons and masters from history, supporting both scholars and enthusiasts to advance their learning, enhance their lives, and make friends in the process.
